You got Computer Science!

Computer Science is a discipline that focuses on the study of computer techniques including computer software, algorithms and how these interact with computer systems. While computer science includes software design and programming it also includes much more than that such as understanding the concepts behind what makes much of our software efficient. Computer Science is also much more broad than just programming as it also includes many fields such as networking, database, machine learning, artifitial intelligence, information security, and data science to name a few.
