Mechanical engineering focuses on the principles and problem-solving techniques of engineering from design to manufacturing. The work of mechanical engineers include analyzing principles of motion, energy, and force in order to create designs that are safe, efficient, and reliable while also being competitive in cost. Mechanical engineers create technology that meets people's everyday needs as they play a role in almost every product that is produced. Mechanical engineers can work in a wide variety of fields due to their versatility. The fields include health care, energy, transporation, space explorations, climate change, and many other fields. To be a mechanical engineer, one must have creativity, as well as knowledge and critical thinking to complete difficult tasks.
