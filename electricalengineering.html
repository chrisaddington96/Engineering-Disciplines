You got Electrical Engineering!

Electrical Engineering is the branch of engineering that deals with the wide range of use cases for electricity. This fields is extremely broad and can cover many different industries such as manufacturing, construction, or power. This can include the use scale of power systems covering large cities or use cases as small as tiny electrical devices. Electrical engineers can be involved in any part of the process for electrical equiptment whether it be deisgn, testing, or managing. The actual work can also vary drastically from fieldwork to design.
